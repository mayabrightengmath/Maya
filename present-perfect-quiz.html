Present Perfect Tense

Read and understand before you take the quiz. (อ่านทำความเข้าใจก่อนทำแบบทดสอบ)

🔤 A. What is Present Perfect Tense?

Present Perfect Tense is used to talk about actions or experiences that happened in the past but are still connected to the present.

ใช้พูดถึงเหตุการณ์ที่เกิดขึ้นในอดีต แต่ยังมีความเกี่ยวข้องหรือผลกระทบต่อปัจจุบัน

Structure: Subject + have / has + Past Participle (V3)
  • I have finished my homework. (ฉันทำการบ้านเสร็จแล้ว — ยังเกี่ยวข้องกับตอนนี้)
  • She has visited Japan.
  • They have lived here for five years.

👥 B. Have / Has — Which One to Use?

Use HAVE with: I · You · We · They

I have seen it.
They have gone home.
Use HAS with: He · She · It

She has finished.
He has eaten lunch.

จำง่าย ๆ: He / She / It → HAS | ที่เหลือทั้งหมด → HAVE

📋 C. Past Participle Verbs (V3)

Regular Verbs (V1 + -ed) play → played
clean → cleaned
watch → watched
finish → finished
visit → visited
Irregular Verbs (must memorize) go → gone
eat → eaten
see → seen
write → written
do → done
take → taken
give → given
come → come

กริยา irregular ต้องจำรูป V3 เพราะไม่ได้เติม -ed ธรรมดา

✅ D. Positive Sentences

Subject + have/has + V3
  • I have eaten breakfast.
  • She has done her homework.
  • We have finished the project.
  • He has written a letter.

❌ E. Negative Sentences

Subject + have/has + NOT + V3
  • I have not seen that movie.
  • He has not finished his work.
have not = haven't has not = hasn't
  • I haven't seen that movie.
  • He hasn't finished his work.

❓ F. Questions

Have / Has + Subject + V3 ?
  • Have you ever been to Japan?
  • Has she finished her homework?
  • Have they started the meeting?

⏰ G. Common Time Expressions

ever never already yet
just since for recently

for = period of time
since = starting point
yet = negatives & questions
already = before expected
just = very recently

  • I have just finished my lunch.
  • She has lived here since 2020.
  • They have studied English for three years.
  • Have you finished your work yet?
  • I have never tried Thai food.
  • Have you ever been to London?
  • She has already eaten dinner.

⚖️ H. Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Present Perfect = ไม่เน้นเวลาที่เกิด หรือยังเกี่ยวข้องกับปัจจุบัน | Past Simple = เกิดและจบไปแล้ว มีเวลาชัดเจน

Present Perfect Past Simple
I have lost my key. (ยังหายอยู่) I lost my key yesterday. (จบแล้ว)
She has visited Paris. (ไม่ระบุเวลา) She visited Paris in 2019. (ระบุปี)
He has finished the report. (ผลยังอยู่) He finished the report two hours ago.

❌ DO NOT use Present Perfect with: yesterday · last week · in 2019 · two hours ago · when I was young

✅ USE Present Perfect with: ever · never · already · yet · just · since · for · recently
