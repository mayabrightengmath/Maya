📋 Answer Key  –  With Explanations
Section A: Grammar (Q1–15)
1.B—"goes" is correct because with "she" (third person singular), we add -s to the verb in the Present Simple.
2.C—"eat" is correct because "they" is plural and does not need -s in the Present Simple.
3.C—"is running" is correct because the action is happening right now (Present Continuous = is/am/are + verb-ing).
4.C—"are watching" is correct because "we" uses "are" in Present Continuous, and the action is happening right now.
5.A—"read" is correct. "Read" is an irregular verb — past tense is also "read" (pronounced "red"). The sentence happened last night.
6.C—"went" is correct because the sentence happened yesterday. "Go" is irregular — past tense is "went."
7.C—"am" is used with "I" in the verb "to be."
8.D—"were" is correct because "they" takes "were" in the past tense of "to be."
9.B—"We" is correct because the subject is "Tom and I," which equals "we."
10.B—"Its" is correct because we use "its" for animals when the gender is not stated.
11.C—"Under" means below something. The cat is hiding below the table.
12.C—"Boxes" is correct. Nouns ending in -x form their plural by adding -es.
13.B—"An" is used before a word starting with a vowel sound. "Elephant" starts with "e."
14.D—"The" is used when both speaker and listener know which specific thing is meant — the door in front of them.
15.C—"Where" asks about a place. The answer would be a location, such as "I went to the park."
Section B: Daily Conversation (Q16–23)
16.C—"I am fine, thank you!" is the correct and polite reply to "How are you?"
17.B—"My name is Lisa." correctly answers "What is your name?"
18.C—"I am 9 years old." correctly answers "How old are you?" — it tells someone your age.
19.C—"I lost my pencil case." is a reason for feeling sad — it answers "What is wrong?"
20.A—"I love pizza!" correctly answers a question about favorite food.
21.A—"Yes, here you go!" is the natural and polite way to agree to lend something.
22.B—"Yes, of course! Follow me." is the best and most helpful reply when someone asks for directions.
23.B—"You are welcome!" is the correct polite response when someone says "Thank you."
Section C: Reading Comprehension (Q24–30)
24.C—"Sam" — the passage starts with "My name is Sam."
25.C—"8 years old" — the passage says "I am 8 years old."
26.D—"Math" — the passage says "My favorite subject is Math."
27.A—"Lily and Tom" — the passage says "Their names are Lily and Tom."
28.B—"At the park" — the passage says "Anna and her family are at the park."
29.C—"Reading a book" — the passage says "Her mom is reading a book."
30.C—"Bright and warm" — "sunny" weather means the sun is shining, so it is bright and warm outside.
