Useful Conversation Rules

Follow these 5 rules to complete conversations correctly every time.

1

Read the whole conversation first.

Before choosing an answer, read the whole conversation. Understanding the situation helps you find the correct response.

2

Look at the situation.

Different situations need different language.

With a teacher → polite language With a close friend → casual language is okay In a shop → formal and polite
3

Check the question word.

What → asking about a thing
Where → asking about a place
When → asking about time
Who → asking about a person
How much → asking about price
Can / Could → asking for help
4

Choose the answer that sounds natural.

AThank you.
BYou're welcome. ✅ (Natural!)

❌ Wrong: "It is 20 baht." — this does not match the situation at all!

5

In games, use short but polite English.

Can you help me? Let's team up. Wait for me! Good game! Follow me.

Strategy

How to Choose the Best Answer

1

After "Thank you" → say "You're welcome."

AThank you.
BYou're welcome. ✅
2

After "How much is it?" → answer with a price.

AHow much is it?
BIt is 50 baht. ✅
3

After "Where are you?" → answer with a place.

AWhere are you?
BI'm at the spawn point. ✅
4

After "Do you want to play?" → accept or refuse.

ADo you want to play?
BYes, that sounds fun! ✅  /  Sorry, I'm busy. ✅
5

If someone asks for help → answer kindly.

AI don't understand this question.
BI can help you. ✅
6

For Roblox → short sentences are okay, but still be polite.

Wait for me!Good game!Let's team up.I'm coming!
7

Check the feeling of the conversation.

😊 Happy😕 Confused🍽️ Hungry📍 Lost🆘 Asking for help

Match your answer to the feeling in the conversation.
